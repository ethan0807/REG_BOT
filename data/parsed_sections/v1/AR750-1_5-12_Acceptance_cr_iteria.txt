A QA and reliability management program will be established and maintained by each Army depot for its 
depot maintenance activities. Output that fails to meet standards will be scrapped, reworked, repaired, or 
otherwise disposed of, as appropriate. U.S. Army depots will — 
a.  Ensure quality requirements are developed and documented early in the life cycle of the weapon 
system.  
b.  Establish cost -effective QA procedures that assure product quality and reliability in maintenance 
shops.  
c.  Provide a ca pability for independent and objective assessment of the quality and reliability of depot 
maintenance output.  
d.  Ensure that only depot maintenance output that meets quality and reliability standards is distributed.
