
5  –  42.  Climate control  
CDC facilities will be operated to conserve energy to the extent possible while meeting the specifications for room climate 
control. CDC personnel will be instructed in energy saving practices. Temperature in all activi ty rooms/ modules occupied 
by children will be set to maintain a range of 68 to 78 ºF. Thermostats will be provided in accordance with the AEI, appendix 
G.
