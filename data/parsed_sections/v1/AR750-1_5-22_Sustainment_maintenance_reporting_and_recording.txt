
5–22.  Sustainment maintenance reporting and recording  
All applicable documents and records will be processed in accordance  with DA  Pam 750  –  8 during depot 
maintenance. Government contractors are required to report work order information for sustainment 
maintenance reset contracts to Army maintenance systems as directed by DCS, G  –  4. Personnel prepar-
ing maintenance contra ct requirements packages will ensure that the provisions of this regulation are in-
cluded in all applicable maintenance contracts.
