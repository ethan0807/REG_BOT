
14–4.  Army Modification Program  
Modifications to Army materiel are either mandatory MWOs that are emergency, urgent, or routine or are 
alternate changes that include minor alterations and special -purpose or special -mission modifications. 
Detailed policy guidance and procedures are outlined in AR 750  –  10, AR 220  –  1, AR 700  –  138, DA Pam 
750  –  1, and DA Pam 738  –  751.
