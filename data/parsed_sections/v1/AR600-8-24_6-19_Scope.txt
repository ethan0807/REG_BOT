
6  –  19.  Scope  
a.  A mandatory retirement is required by law and is initiated by HQDA. An officer must be retired on the date 
established by the applicable statute unless specifically provided by law (see 10 USC 640). An officer may request 
retirement and be retired voluntar ily on mandatory retirement date.  
b.  The address of the office responsible for a mandatory retirement of a general officer is General Officer Manage-
ment Office, Office of the Chief of Staff, Army, 200 Army Pentagon, Washington, DC 20310  –  0200.
