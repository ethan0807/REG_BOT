
6  –  42.  Facilities requirements 
a.  Occupancy approval.   Family housing units authorized for dependent habitation may be considered for use as FCC 
homes. Each individual housing unit must receive certificat ion to be used as an FCC home.  
b.  Structural changes.   No structural changes including the removal/addition of walls, flooring and major construction 
work may be made in quarters used for FCC homes for the provision of child care without the permission of  the DEH.  
c.  Identification card.   An 81⁄2 by 11– inch identification card containing the information in DA Label 176 will be used 
to identify family housing units used as FCC homes and will be placed in a window that is observable from the street.
