
4–26.  Provisi ons of inter -service support agreements  
The ISSAs will — 
a.  Specify responsibilities for furnishing repair parts and other support materials required for the com-
pletion of the maintenance operations.  
b.  Make suitable provisions for the interchange of mai ntenance performance and management data 
between all parties to the agreement.  
c.  Contain provisions for a review every 2 years to determine whether the agreement should be contin-
ued, modified, or terminated.  
d.  Review  the funding annex and cost estimates annually to determine actual billed costs in accord-
ance with DoDI 4000.19.
