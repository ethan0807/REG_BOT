
5  –  25.  Facility designation  
All facilities housing CDS center –based programs (with exception of separate SAS and SPS parent co –op programs) will 
be identified as child de velopment centers. The installation name geographic location of the facility may be used for public 
identification purposes (e.g., Smith village CDC). Terms such as nursery, child care center, preschool and unique names 
such as Kiddie Kastle will not be us ed to designate facilities.
