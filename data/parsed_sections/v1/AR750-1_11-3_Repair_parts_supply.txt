
11–3.  Repair parts supply  
a.  Repair parts for an NTV are obtained under the provisions of AR 710  –  2. 
b.  Cannibalization of uneconomically reparable Army owned NTVs prior to turn in to DLA  –  DS is au-
thorized if serviceable parts are deemed mission critical and not available by local vendor or through LIS. 
c.  Authorization of controlled exchange for repair parts on Army owned NTVs is only authorized in ac-
cordance with paragraph 4  –  10 in this regulation when repair parts are not available by local vendor or 
LIS.
