
6  –  8.  Retirement statu s 
a.  An RA officer placed on the retired list continues to be an officer of the U.S. Army.  
b.  Unless there is evidence of fraud, an officer’s retirement under a statute exhausts the power of the President and 
SECARMY. The record of Executive action canno t be revoked or modified to make the retirement relate to another 
statute, even though more than one statute properly applied at the time of retirement. No member of the Army may be 
ordered to AD solely for the purpose of qualifying for retired pay for non regular service (see 10 USC 12737).
