
1  –  5.  Records management (recordkeeping) requirements  
The records manage ment requirement for all record numbers, associated forms, and reports required by this regulation 
are addressed in the Army Records Retention Schedule -Army (RRS  –  A). Detailed information for all related record 
numbers, forms, and reports are located in A RIMS/RRS  –  A at https://www.arims.army.mil. If any record numbers, 
forms, and reports are not current, addressed, and/or published correctly in ARIMS/RRS  –  A, see DA Pam 25  –  403 for 
guidance.
