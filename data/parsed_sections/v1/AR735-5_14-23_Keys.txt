a. When a person loses a key due to negligence or willful misconduct, they will be assessed financial liability for
the lost key.
b. When the loss of a key through negligence or willful misconduct is the proximate cause of replacing a locking
device or door by rendering the locking device or door unsuitable for its intended purpose, the respondent will be heldfinancially liable for the replacement cost of the locking device and/or the door. Replacement cost includes both thecost of the parts and the cost of the labor to install.
