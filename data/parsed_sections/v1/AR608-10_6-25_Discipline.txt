
6  –  25.  Discipline  
All FCC providers will prepare a written discipline and touch policy based on the criteria in paragraph 4  –  10 prior to 
provision of care.  
a.  This policy will be given to all parents of children enrolling in FCC homes as part of the admission process.  
b.  The policy will clearly state which disciplinary techniques will be used and must be approved by FCC management 
personnel. Corporal punishment is not allowed.
