
1  –  11.  Principles of support 
The military personnel system will direct a function to — 
a.  Provide a mechanism to terminate the services of an officer prior to the terms of the original contract (both 
voluntarily and involuntarily).  
b.  Provide authority to transfer officers from one component to another.  
c.  Provide authority to discharge officers from all military obligations.  
d.  Support the Army’s personnel life -cycle function of transition.
