
15–1.  General guidance  
The Army conducts activities to restore the Army’s personnel and equipment to a desired level of combat 
capability commensurate with future missions and maintains accurate visibility over equipment repair, 
 
replacement, recapitalization and expenditures in order to sustain equipment availability and meet opera-
tional requirements. Equipment reset is a subset process for field and sustainment maintenance within 
the Army RESET of the SRM strategy. AR 525  –  29 contains specifics regarding SRM.
