
14–30. Subsistence accounting variances
a. Troop issue subsistence activity.(1) Adjust unidentifiable operational accounting variances in the TISA account (ration breakdown for ARNG) that
do not exceed the authorized tolerances according to AR 30–22. The approving authority for DA Form 4170 willapprove the gain and loss variances.
(2) Prepare a DD Form 200 according to chapter 13 when the unidentifiable accounting losses exceed the tolerance
authorized by AR 30–22, or if the approving authority for DA Form 4170 does not approve the gains or losses.
b. Overdrawn or under drawn meal status, Army National Guard. Bring overdrawn or under drawn meal status in
excess of the authorized tolerance factors for annual training or inactive duty training into tolerance per AR 30–22.
