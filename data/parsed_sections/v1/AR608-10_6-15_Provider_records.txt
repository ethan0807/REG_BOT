
6  –  15.  Provider records  
Individual files will be maintained for each provider and will include — 
a.  Name and address, telephone number, training, and other related experiences and qualifications.  
b.  Employment reference, training requirement, and reports of medical examinations.  
c.  Appraisals, awards, and promotions and grievance actions.  
d.  Notifications of personnel actions.  
e.  FCC certification document.  
f.  FCC provider health records.  
g.  Statement of understanding.  
h.  Background check information.  
i.  Training and education records.  
j.  Files of children seriously injured during care.  
k.  Complaints received and  documentation of follow up of complaints.  
l.  Receipt showing payment of NAF claims costs.
