
4–3.  Low Usage Program maintenance plans  
a.  Equipment without an approved NCOMP may be placed in the L ow Usage Program ( LUP) at the 
commander’s discretion in this regulation and DA Pam 750  –  1, if they meet the low -usage criteria. The im-
plementation of LUP does not relieve commanders of the responsibility for adequate maintenance of their 
equipment.  
b.  For mandatory procedures relating to the LUP and specific criteria for equipment to be placed in a 
low-usage status see DA Pam 750  –  1.
