
7–2. Nonexpendable property
Nonexpendable property is personal property that is not consumed in use and that retains its original identity during the
period of use. This includes all nonconsumable major end items authorized by DA-recognized authorization documents(see AR 71–32). These items have an ARC of “N” contained in FEDLOG. Commercial and fabricated items similar toitems coded “N” in FEDLOG are considered nonexpendable property.
