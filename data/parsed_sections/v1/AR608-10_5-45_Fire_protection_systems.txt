
5  –  45.  Fire protection systems  
a.  Fire alarm and detection systems will be installed per AEI, appendix  G. Battery operated or single station smoke 
detectors are not acceptable. All building alarms will transmit a signal to the fire department or fire alarm communication 
center serving the facility (bi –lingual operators required in overseas locations).  
b.  An automatic sprinkler system and fire separation const ruction will be provided for all hazard areas according to 
AEI, appendix  G.
