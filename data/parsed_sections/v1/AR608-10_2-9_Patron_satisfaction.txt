 –  9.  Patron satisfaction  
a.  Written guidelines will be established addressing procedures for parents who wish to submit a complaint or concern.  
b.  Program directors in all CDS delivery systems will conduct patron satisfaction surveys, at a minimum on an annual 
basis; and establish a follow –up plan for addressing areas of dissatisfaction as appropriate.  
c.  Patron satisfaction survey formats have been developed for installation use and are available from USACFSC 
(CFSC  –  SF  –  CY).
