
5–1. General transfer of accountability policy
This chapter prescribes policy and procedures for making administrative transfers of property accountability and
responsibility by other than ordinary physical issue and receipt transactions. Excluded are transfers of propertyaccountability and responsibility to the following:
a. Commissary officers.
b. Troop issue subsistence officers.
c. Reserve Components when not in Federal service, except as specified in AR 710–2.
d. Contractors who are furnished or have acquired Government property.
e. Army military clothing sales stores operated by the Army and Air Force Exchange System.
