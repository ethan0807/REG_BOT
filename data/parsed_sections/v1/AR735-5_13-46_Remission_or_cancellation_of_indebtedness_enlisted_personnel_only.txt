
13–46. Remission or cancellation of indebtedness (enlisted personnel only)
When financial liability assessed through a financial liability investigation causes financial hardship on an enlisted
Soldier, they may submit an application for remission or cancellation of the debt, DA Form 3508 (Application forRemission or Cancellation of Indebtedness) through their commander, per AR 600–4. A copy of the approved DDForm 200 assessing financial liability will be submitted with the application.
