
11–5. Intent
The purpose of the CSDP is to—
a. Establish supply discipline as regulatory guidance as follows:
(1) Standardize supply discipline requirements.(2) Provide responsible personnel with a single listing of all existing supply discipline requirements.(3) Make the Army more efficient regarding time spent monitoring subordinates’ actions.b. To achieve the above purpose, the CSDP will—
(1) Ensure compliance with DA supply policy and procedures.(2) Determine the adequacy of established DA supply policy and procedures.(3) Identify supply problems to permit timely corrective action within the chain of command.
