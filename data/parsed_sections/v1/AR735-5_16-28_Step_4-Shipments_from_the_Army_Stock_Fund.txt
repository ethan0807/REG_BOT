
16–28. Step 4–Shipments from the Army Stock Fund
DD Form 361s initiated for shipments from the AWCF according to paragraph 16–12 (covering step 3) will be
processed in step 4 in the same way as the other Army DD Form 361 except change of the accounting classificationshown for deposit of proceeds to that of the AWCF consignor prescribed in DFAS–IN Regulation 37–1. Thisrequirement applies to all AWCF transportation discrepancies including those amounting to less than $100 per line.
