
6–9.  Items removed from crash -damaged aircraft  
Pending the outcome of an aircraft investigation, in accordance with AR 385  –  10 and DA Pam 385  –  90, 
extreme caution will be exercised in the reuse of items removed from crash -damaged aircraft or aircraft 
that have been involved in accidents. Items removed from a crashed aircraft or an aircraft that has been 
involved in an accident will not be reused regardless of apparent serviceability until such items have been 
subjected to a thorough inspection in accordance with paragraphs 6  –  8 and 6  –  10.
