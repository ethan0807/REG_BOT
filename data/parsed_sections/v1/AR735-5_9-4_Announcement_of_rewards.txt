
9–4. Announcement of rewards
a. Notices of rewards will read as shown in figure 9–1 and are to be used in the daily bulletin, post bulletin board,
or hand flyers.
b. Any information that might lead to the recovery of lost property will be included in the notice.
c. Notice of rewards may list one or more items.
d. Distribution of announcements offering a reward will be sufficiently broad to ensure reaching all persons and
organizations reasonably expected to help recover the property.
