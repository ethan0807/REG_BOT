 –  30.  Administrative health procedures  
a.  Settings designated as sick child options may  accept children excluded from usual care setting upon written recom-
mendation of a health care professional and after caregiving employees and FCC providers have received specialized 
training in care of children with contagious diseases.  
b.  CDS personnel will report outbreaks of communicable diseases per paragraph  2–20b. 
c.  Program directors, assistant directors, front desk personnel, caregiving employees, and FCC providers will receive 
training in preventative health measures.
