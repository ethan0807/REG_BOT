
14–19.  Maintenance messages  
Maintenance messages (maintenance, sustainment, logistics supply, technical, or operational) that are 
not related to safety can provide new or different pertinent non -safety information (see AR 750  –  6). Prior 
to publishing, all maintenance advisory messages will be coordinat ed through the appropriate AMC com-
modity command safety office to ensure their content is not safety related. The only authorized method of 
informing ACOMs, ASCCs, and DRUs of hazardous equipment conditions is through the Ground Safety 
Notification Sys tem.
