
14–7.  Battlefield damage assessment and repair  
a.  The purpose of BDAR  is to return disabled equipment rapidly to combat or to enable the equipment 
to self -recover. BDAR is the commander’s responsibility, based on mission, enemy, terrain and weather, 
troops and support available, time available, and civil considerations and  is accomplished by the operator 
and/or crew and field maintenance personnel. Realistic training must be performed during peacetime to 
ensure wartime proficiency.  
b.  For mandatory BDAR procedures see DA Pam 750  –  1.
