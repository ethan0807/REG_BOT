
1  –  19.  Officer awaiting appellate review of adjudged dismissal or dishonorable discharge  
An officer who has been convicted and sentenced to dismissal or dishonorable discharge will not be discharged prior 
to completion of appellate review without prior approval of CG, HRC. An RA officer may be processed for excess 
leave under AR 600  –  8  –  10. An RC officer may be released from AD in accordance with paragraph 2  –  18.
