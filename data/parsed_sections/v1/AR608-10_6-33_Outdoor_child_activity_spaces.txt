 –  33.  Outdoor child activity spaces  
a.  Children enrolled in FCC homes will have access to hazard –free outdoor play space. Where such play space is not 
available on the premises, parks, or playgrounds, other easily accessible outdoor spaces may be used with approval of the FCC director.  
b.  Children under 5 years will not be permitted outdoors unless accompanied by the FCC provider.  
c.  Portable/removable fencing to enclose small play areas for young children ma y be provided as part of the FCC 
toy/resource lending library.  
d.  Children enrolled in an FCC home should spend part of each day, weather permitting, outside.
