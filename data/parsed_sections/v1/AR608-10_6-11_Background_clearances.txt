
6  –  11.  Background clearances  
The following background clearance and reference checks will be done on all FCC pr ovider applicants, family members 
over 12 years of age, and other authorized adults residing in the home, and substitute providers (see chap 2 –24). 
a.  Army Central Registry.  
b.  Local Military Police.  
c.  CID records check to include Defense Central Investigation Index (DC11).  
d.  Civilian law records check where feasible.  
e.  Alcohol & Drug Prevention Program.  
f.  Sponsor’s commander.  
g.  Three personal references.
