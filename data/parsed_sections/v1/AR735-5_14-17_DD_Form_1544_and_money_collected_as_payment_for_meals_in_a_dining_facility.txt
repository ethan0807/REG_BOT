
14–17. DD Form 1544 and money collected as payment for meals in a dining facility
DD Form 1544 (Cash Meal Payment Book) records losses of cash meal payment sheets and/or money collected as
payment for meals in a dining facility require an investigation under AR 15–6 per AR 30–22. A DD Form 200 will beinitiated to account for the lost form(s) and/or money, and to assess financial liability if negligence is found.
